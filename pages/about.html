About PawCare

Together, we protect the voiceless — one paw at a time.

Our Story

PawCare began with a simple act of compassion — helping a single stray dog in need. From that moment, our founders realized the urgent need to protect thousands of unvaccinated strays across cities and villages. Established in 2020, PawCare has since grown into a network of volunteers, veterinarians, and donors united by one vision — to ensure every dog receives the care and vaccination they deserve.

Why Vaccination?

Every year, thousands of stray dogs suffer from preventable diseases like rabies and parvovirus. Vaccination not only saves their lives but also ensures community safety by preventing disease spread. Through awareness programs and vaccination drives, PawCare bridges the gap between compassion and action.

  • Prevents deadly diseases
  • Promotes community health
  • Builds immunity and longevity
  • Encourages humane animal care
